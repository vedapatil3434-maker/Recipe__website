Featured Recipe: Vegetable Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 Cucumber
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Lemon
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Wash all vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Cut them into small pieces.
  3. Mix everything in a bowl.
  4. Add lemon juice and salt.
  5. Serve fresh.
